Shannon McGovern

Web Developer, Social Media Manager & Virtual Assistant

Based in the UK

My name is Shannon, and I’m from a coastal town in Scotland called Dunbar. I had always known I wanted to leave my home and see the world, and in 2015 I fled the nest to live in Australia for two years and have been obsessed with travel ever since. I was living a life of returning home, working constantly, saving money, travelling until my money ran out and starting over again.

In 2021, I moved back home from what I hoped would be a permanent move to Thailand due to the Pandemic. I put all my energy into my remote job as an ESL teacher. I built up my regular students and continuously filled every slot on my schedule. Although I was finally making good money to support myself while travelling once the pandemic was over, I realised it wouldn’t give me the freedom I craved, and I was constantly feeling burnt out.

I decided to take a massive leap of faith, and I invested in myself. I worked with a digital nomad coach who introduced me to the term ‘Virtual Assistant’ and it all sort of clicked. I threw all my energy into starting my own remote VA business.

I soon realised it was about more than just allowing me to work and travel. It has allowed me to make new friends and build a community. I get to support other business owners by giving them time for things such as taking on new clients, hosting events or even just spending time with family. I get to be creative and help bring others’ visions to life, which I love. It has given me time to spend with my family, puppers and friends. I have time to take courses and learn new skills.
