How can I begin the process?

The first step is book your free 30-minute consultation call.

This is where you will share all about your business, what kind of support would benefit you such as social media management, content creation or admin task and ask any questions you may have about what it is like to work with a Virtual Assistant.

Alternativly, you can give all this information in a enquirety form and then I will be in touch with you.

Both can be found in the link in my bio.

What happens next?

You will receive a personalised business proposal that give more in depth details about how I run my business and how I will support you and eliminate pain points based on the information shared previously.

At this point you can decided wether you would like to work together.

Is it legitimate?

Of course! Before we start working together, you will receive a contract with full terms and conditions that you can read through thoroughly. Only once we have a mutual agreement on how our business relationship will work, will we move forward.

How does communication work?

All communication is done through Slack.

My business hours will be discussed before hand and I will make myself avaiavle to you between these times for any extra support you need.

If we are working on a retainer basis, I will also be happy to organise monthly check in calls on with Zoom or Google Meets (whichever is your preference) at start of each month to touch base, where we can discuss how the previous month went and if there are any alterations/improvements we can make for the month ahead.

How are tasks managed?

All project management is done through an app called Trello.

Trello will be your main area to share tasks, view and approve content, access shared folders and view your social media strategy and schedule.

Trello is easy to use and a great way for your to get a visualisation of how your package is being used.

I will offer a free walkthrough of the app if you feel this will be helpful.

Do I have to share passwords?

You do not have to share any confidential information that you do not want to.

Last Pass is a service that allows you to share access to accounts such as social media accounts or emails without sharing the log in information.

How are hours tracked?

I use an app called Toggl Track which allows me to time and keep track of hours used throughout the month. This means you are paying for pure productivity.

Hours will be updated weekly on Trello.
